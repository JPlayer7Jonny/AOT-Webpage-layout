Season 1 (Spoilers)

No. of episodes: 25

It all began on the day of the Colossal Titan attack in the Shinganshina District, where a Smiling Titan ate Eren’s mother. After joining the Cadet Corps, Eren’s first battle is undeniably disastrous since he gets eaten by the Bearded Titan. But when he lands in the Titans stomach he’s enraged at the carnage the monsters have caused, ultimately transforming into one himself and bursting out of the Bearded Titan to slaughter the attacking monsters.

Eren, Armin and Mikasa all wind up joining the Survey Corps under Levi Ackerman and Erwin Smith. But a new enemy emerges when the two titans that had been captured and were being was experimenting on are mysteriously murdered by someone using omni-directional gear.

It’s later revealed that Annie is the devastating Female Titan, and uses a bladed ring to reveal herself when Erwin and Armin confront her. After remembering all the pain the Titans have caused, Eren fights her in his monstrous form and savagely beats her while causing absolute chaos in the Stohess District. Before he can eat Annie, she crystalizes herself and is locked away by the Scout Regiment. The first season ends with a truly shocking revelation that there are Titans buried inside Wall Sina.

Season 2 (Spoilers)

No. of episodes: 12

The season begins with the Survey Corps trying to find out what are the secrets behind the walls, and a new titan is introduced, the Beast Titan. After that titan is introduced, showing off the devastation it can cause, the series delves into the political history of the world through Christa Lenz.

A flashback reveals Christa’s real name is Historia Reiss and she’s the illegitimate child of Rod Reiss, the King of Eldia. During the shocking sixth episode, the fellow recruit and Eren's friend Reiner breaks down in exhaustion and reveals to Eren that he’s the Armoured Titan and Bertholdt is the terrifying Colossal Titan. He also reviews that they want to take eren to wherever they came from.

As the Scouts fight Titans being thrown by Reiner’s Armoured Titan, Eren winds up facing the Smiling Titan that ate his mother way back at the beginning of the series. In his anger, he unlocks a mysterious ability called the Founding Titan, allowing Eren to control any and all Titans. He uses it on those nearby and forces them to eat the Smiling Titan. The second season ends with the Beast Titan on Wall Maria while a mysterious blonde man says “Not just yet.”

Season 3 - Part 1 (Spoilers)

No. of episodes: 10

The first half of the third season starts with Eren's friends recruit group becoming the elite squad of the Survey Corps, the Levi Squad. That happened after all members of the squad but Levi being killed by the Female Titan. The Survey corps is required to hand over Eren because of the coleteral damage of the fight against the Female Titan.

In the meantime, Eren and Historia are kidnapped by Rod Reiss, who plan and taking back the Founding Titan power, which had been always been with the royal family, but somehow ended up with Eren. Reiss plans to passing that power to Historia by making her eat Eren.

Meanwhile, the Commander Erwin and Squad Levi try to overthrow the corrupt royal Government headed by Rod Reiss, since Historia is the rightful heir to the throne, and by doing so, rescueing Eren. Before being rescued, Eren learns that his father, Grisha, is the one who turned him into a Titan and a buried memory reveals Eren quickly ate his father after that first transformation. At the end of the part 1 of the season, Reiss turns himself into a titan, but they manage to kill him. Then it ends with Historia being crowned Queen.

Season 3 - part 2 (Spoilers)

No. of episodes: 12

After Eren learning how to use the titan hardening ability, the Survey Corps goes to back to Shinganshina with the mission of sealing the holes on the Wall Maria, however the Reiner, Bertholdt and the Beast Titan are waiting for them. The Beast Titan revealed to be Zeke Jaeger, Eren’s half-brother – who claims to be a victim of their father, Grisha. He’s trying to capture Eren so Marley has the Coordinate power to control Titans.

During the same battle to take back the Shinganshina District, Armin distracts Bernholdt’s Colossal Titan so Eren can sneakily rip the villain out of his monstrous form. It comes at a price as Armin’s burnt alive and close to death. But after he’s revived by turning him into the Colossal Titan shifter.

They get to Eren father's basement, and found out that they actually have been living on a island, and that the rest of the world is far more technological advanced, and that the most powerful nation, Marley, is using the titan shifters power to rule. Also they are after the Founding Titan power, because it grants the power to the user to release and control the thousands of Colossal titans that are inside the walls.

In the second part of the season, aware of the global anti-Eldian sentiment resulting from Marleyan propaganda, and the emergency of being attack by them, Eren Jaeger tries to meet Zeke, so he can use the Founding Titan power to win the war with Rumbling, unleashing thousands of Colossal Titans in a widespread effort beyond the island. Even though everyone in the island is divided and suspicious of Eren's intentions, they have to unite and fight to protect their homeland.

The half season starts right into a fierce battle, with several casaulties on both sides, and Eren is fighting the three Marley Titans alone. Mikasa, Armin and the other comrades try to go help Eren, but they got caught up in combat with the enemy soldiers, being unable to reach Eren. Then, the Beast Titan, who survived while Levi also escaped, arrives at Shiganshina and start throwing rocks at the enimies and their airships.

Now Eren has to fight with all his strength to reach Zeke. The whole fate of himself, of the Eldian and even the whole world will be determined by what happens next The 12 episodes of Final Season part 2 are not fully released yet, and episodes are until coming. A lot can still happen.
